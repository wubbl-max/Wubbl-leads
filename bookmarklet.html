How to use

  1. Drag the blue "+ Add to Pipeline" button to your Arc toolbar.
  2. Go to LinkedIn (Feed or Search results).
  3. Click "+ Add to Pipeline" from your toolbar to activate.
  4. Hover any post — a blue + button appears in the top-right corner.
  5. Click it — a popup opens with auto-filled URL, name, and ICP score.
  6. Hit Save — lead appears instantly in your Pipeline tab.
Note: Leads are saved via an iframe bridge to your GitHub Pages domain. Both the bookmarklet and the app must use the same GitHub username above.
