LinkedIn Prospector Bookmarklet

One-click extension. Configure your GitHub username, drag to bookmarks, and start prospecting directly on LinkedIn.

This ensures saved leads are routed to your deployed GitHub Pages domain (https://<username>.github.io/linkedin-prospecting-tool/)

Drag the blue button above to your browser's bookmarks bar

How to use

  1. Type your GitHub username above.
  2. Drag the blue "＋ Add to Pipeline" button to your bookmarks bar.
  3. Browse LinkedIn (Feed or Search page).
  4. Click "＋ Add to Pipeline" in your bookmarks bar to activate hover chips.
  5. Hover over any post to see the blue "＋" icon in the top right.
  6. Click "＋" to open a pre-filled scraping modal, and click **Save**.
Note: Data is synchronized using a secure iframe messaging bridge. Leads saved on LinkedIn will appear in the pipeline on your deployed GitHub Pages domain.
