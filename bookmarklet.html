Drag the blue button above to your browser's bookmarks bar

How to use

  1. Drag the blue button above to your bookmarks bar
  2. Browse LinkedIn and open any post you're interested in
  3. Click "+ Add to Pipeline" in your bookmarks bar
  4. A form will pop up — fill in the poster's title and ICP score
  5. Click Save — it's logged in your pipeline
  6. Open index.html → Pipeline tab to review it
Note: The pipeline data is stored in your browser's localStorage. Both this bookmarklet and index.html must be opened in the same browser (same profile) for the data to be shared.
